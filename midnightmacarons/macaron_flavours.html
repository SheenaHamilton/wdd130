What is a Macaron?

For starters...they're amazing! Once you've tried one of these amazing delicacies, you'll be hooked. I was, that's how I started down the path of perfecting these little goodies, and let me tell you, it was no easy task. They may look simple but are extremely picky to bake. After many trials and MANY failed batches I can finally say I've perfected my process. But enough about that...What is a macaron?

A macaron (mack-a-ROHN) is a confection made with almond flour to make two round, flat cookies. These delicate cookies sandwich a filling. Flavours and colors can be any variation. I do love to try new combinations!
