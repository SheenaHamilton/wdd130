Welcome!

Welcome to Midnight Macarons, a little bit about myself, I'm a mom to 3 wonderful little girls, Brynn and my twins Lennon and Margot, I also work part time as a dental hygienist and part time stay at home mom. I learned the art of baking Macarons during maternity leave with my first child Brynn. Most times with kids it can be hard to find a moment during the day to work on my art and therefore, the reason for “Midnight”. Oftentimes, it is midnight when I'm baking.

I love to make Macarons, and I'm so excited to be able to share this delicious treat with you! I hope you love them as much as I do!
